How Your HI Grade™ is Calculated

Every company receives a score from 0-100 across five dimensions — Human Consciousness, Understanding & Empathy, Moral & Ethical Conduct, Alive & Environmental, and Natural Transparency. These are averaged into a composite HI Grade™.

Scores are computed from six public data sources: SEC EDGAR filings, EPA environmental records, BLS wage data, CDP climate disclosures, job posting analysis, and Glassdoor employee ratings. No private data is used without your consent.

The methodology is open source. You can audit every formula, verify every data source, and understand exactly why you scored what you did.

Your HI Grade™ isn't a judgment — it's a roadmap. Each dimension tells you exactly where the balance is off and what to improve.

HI. API

Integrate HI Grades™ into your products. Procurement platforms, investment tools, ESG dashboards.

REST API

The HI Score API provides programmatic access to HI Grades™ for 500+ companies. JSON responses, no authentication required for public endpoints. Rate limited to 60 requests per minute.

Base URL: https://hi-api-production.up.railway.app

Endpoints

GET/api/v1/score/{domain}Score by domain
GET/api/v1/score/ticker/{ticker}Score by stock ticker
GET/api/v1/search?q={query}Search companies
GET/api/v1/grades/top?limit=10Top rated companies
GET/api/v1/grades/bottom?limit=10Bottom rated companies
GET/api/v1/statsDatabase statistics
GET/api/v1/healthHealth check

About HI.

Find the HI balance.

HI — Human Intelligence.

The greeting. The counter to AI. The question every consumer should ask: is this company powered by human intelligence, or just artificial? HI isn't anti-AI. It's the reminder that human consciousness, empathy, and ethics are the intelligence that matters most.

HI. measures the balance between humans and technology at every company. We score organizations across five dimensions — the things AI can't be: conscious, empathetic, ethical, alive, and transparent.

Every company gets a HI Grade™ — a letter grade from HI Certified to F. The pass/fail line is 42 — the answer to life, the universe, and everything.

We're not anti-AI. We're pro-balance. Technology that empowers humans scores well. Technology that replaces and hides them scores poorly.

The scoring methodology is open source (Apache 2.0). Anyone can audit the math, verify the data, and hold us accountable.

The HUMAN Framework

🧠
H — Human Consciousness
Creative agency, craft, accountability vs. AI automation
💙
U — Understanding & Empathy
Genuine care vs. simulated empathy
⚖️
M — Moral & Ethical Conduct
Principled action vs. optimization-at-all-costs
🌍
A — Alive & Environmental
True ecological cost including AI infrastructure
🔍
N — Natural Transparency
Honest disclosure vs. humanwashing

Data Sources

HI Grades are computed from six free, public data sources: SEC EDGAR, EPA ECHO, Bureau of Labor Statistics, CDP climate disclosures, job posting analysis, and Glassdoor employee ratings.

All scores are estimated from public data. Verified scores require HI Certification.

Open Source

The scoring methodology, browser extension, and community tools are open source on GitHub. A transparency framework that hides its own methodology would be hypocritical.
